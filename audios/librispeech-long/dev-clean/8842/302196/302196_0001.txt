BOCCACCIO IN HIS LIFE OF DANTE TELLS US THAT THE GREAT POET IN LATER LIFE WAS ASHAMED OF THIS WORK OF HIS YOUTH. NOR IS THIS PERHAPS ITS LEAST PRAISE. A WORD SHOULD BE SAID HERE OF THE TITLE OF DANTE'S AUTOBIOGRAPHY. THE ADJECTIVE NUOVO NUOVA OR NOVELLO NOVELLA LITERALLY NEW IS OFTEN USED BY DANTE AND OTHER EARLY WRITERS IN THE SENSE OF YOUNG. THIS HAS INDUCED SOME EDITORS OF THE VITA NUOVA TO EXPLAIN THE TITLE AS MEANING EARLY LIFE
