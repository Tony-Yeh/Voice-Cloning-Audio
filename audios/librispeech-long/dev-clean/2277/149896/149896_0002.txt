THERE WAS A DOOR WHICH OPENED UNDER THE STEPS INTO THE KITCHEN PROTECTED BY AN IRON GRATING INTENDED AS A SAFEGUARD AGAINST BURGLARS. HE RANG THE BELL AND THEN WAITED. FINALLY SEEING THAT NO ONE WAS COMING HE TURNED AND WENT BACK TO HIS CAB. I GUESS THEY'VE GONE OUT HE SAID APOLOGETICALLY TO THE INDIVIDUAL WHO WAS HIDING HIS RED FACE IN A LOOSE TARPAULIN RAIN COAT
